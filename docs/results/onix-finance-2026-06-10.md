**Executive Summary**  
The US mid‑market commercial banking and regional credit‑union IT outsourcing market is expanding, driven by talent gaps, legacy‑system pressures, and a strong push for AI‑enabled automation and cloud migration. Two‑thirds of credit unions have considered outsourcing IT to address talent shortages, with nearly one‑third already using it as a tactic in 2023【1】. The global banking back‑office outsourcing market is forecast to grow from $16.8 bn in 2025 to $25.0 bn by 2035 at a 4.1 % CAGR, while the middle‑office outsourcing segment is projected to add $4.77 bn at a 10.8 % CAGR from 2024‑2029, both fueled by cost‑reduction, AI/automation, and regulatory compliance demands【2】【3**. Enterprise‑scale providers such as Accenture, Infosys, and TCS dominate the landscape, but a 250‑person nearshore firm like Onix does not compete directly for the same mega‑deals; instead it can target mid‑market opportunities where its AI, cloud‑native, and legacy‑modernization strengths align with buyer needs. Onix’s fintech vertical experience and US/UK client base give it a foothold, but it lacks proven banking/credit‑union case studies and specific compliance certifications, which must be addressed to win trust in this conservative vertical.

**Market Trends**  
- **Credit‑union IT outsourcing sentiment**: While 67 % of credit unions say they’ve considered outsourcing IT and 41 % consider both operations and information security, credit unions are not eager to replace or augment staff with technology; however, 32 % reported outsourcing or augmenting staff as a tactic in 2023【1】 (Source: Wipfli, 2024) [1].  
- **Banking back‑office outsourcing market growth**: The Global Banking Back Office Outsourcing Market is expected to exhibit a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 4.1 % from 2025 to 2035, driven by a growing need for banks to reduce operational costs and improve efficiency. Technological advancements, especially in artificial intelligence and automation, are transforming operational processes in banking back‑office functions, leading to higher precision and reduced turnaround times【2】 (Source: WiseGuyReports, 2026) [2].  
- **Middle‑office outsourcing market expansion**: The middle‑office outsourcing market size is valued to increase by **USD 4.77 billion**, at a **CAGR of 10.8 %** from 2024 to 2029. Need for new technologies and budget constraints will drive the middle‑office outsourcing market【3】 (Source: Technavio, 2025) [3].
